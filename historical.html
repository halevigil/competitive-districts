Historical Distribution of US House District Presidential Margins, 2008–2024

For each cycle, the histogram of two-party presidential margin (R% − D%, in percentage points) across the 435 House districts on the map in effect that November. Bin width is configurable in historical_config.js. Same visual style as the simulator's district-partisanship histogram, so you can compare against the model's output.
Sources: Daily Kos / Downballot "Presidential results by congressional district" CSVs. Convention: bin centred on integer c covers margins in [c−1, c+1). Mean and median are unweighted across the 435 districts (not weighted by votes cast).
