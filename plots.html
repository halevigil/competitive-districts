Mismatch Distribution by Magnitude

Average count of "mismatched" reps per chamber, binned by signed district partisanship. Republicans winning D-lean districts appear on the left (negative partisanship), Democrats winning R-lean districts appear on the right (positive partisanship). Heights are averages across many simulations.

Mismatches per District (rate)

Same as above but normalised: at each partisanship bin, the average number of mismatches divided by the number of districts at that partisanship. Effectively the conditional probability that a district at that partisanship elects the "wrong-party" representative.
